"Donated $1310!!"

"The concept is quite simple. We choose a LOCAL charity each month an instead of charging you $5-$10 for a watch battery and installation, we encourage a donation to a local organization in need. Other services such as watchband sizing, cleaning, polishing, inspection of rings, and other small repairs are also done at no charge. Not only does this program raise money for charities but it also raises awareness of the needs of charitable groups within our community. Along with the donation canister, literature about the organization is prominently displayed and available for customers to review. This unique approach enables our customers to donate and learn about the special needs of our community. By getting a free watch battery at Waterfall Jewelers, you are helping support our local community and charities in need!"
