Grant Recipients

The Five Points of Hope Community Cancer Care fund assists with expenses such as deductibles, coinsurance, co-pays, prescriptions, medical equipment/supplies and other expenses deemed to be burdensome by a licensed social worker.

Financial Assistance is granted and executed based as follows:

  • Individual in need has been diagnosed with cancer
  • Financial aid is needed as a result of the cancer diagnosis
  • The individual is a Michigan Resident
  • The individual is a legal United States of America (US) Resident
  • Maximum $500 grant annually per individual
  • Request was forwarded by a licensed social worker PLEASE have your Oncololgy Social Worker email grants@fivepointsofhope.com for a copy of the criteria list and application
  • Grant request decisions will be emailed to the requesting licensed social worker
  • Payment will be made directly to the vendor(s)

About Us

Five Points Of Hope Cancer Care Fund is a Michigan-based non-profit organization dedicated to assisting individuals experiencing financial difficulties while dealing with the diagnosis of cancer.
